Competition in Smartphone Innovation

The smartphone market has always been an oligopoly, as phone production was so difficult. Motorolla and AT&T had extreme control over the market but as time passed their technology became heavily outdated and was overwhelmed by Apple and Samsung. The latter companies owning 14% and 20% mmarket share respectively.
