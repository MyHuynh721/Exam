2G Introduced

2G is the second generation of cellular networks, based on the Global System for Mobile Communication (GSM). It was designed to accommodate the growing number of mobile phones, 2G introduced cellular services like SMS, multimedia messaging, and digitally encrypted voice conversations. It made more efficient use of the radio frequency (RF) spectrum, so more devices could share the same frequency bands.
